About Labour Finder

Labour Finder is a digital platform designed to connect daily wage workers with owners who need skilled and unskilled labour. Our goal is to make hiring and job searching fast, easy, and reliable.

Workers can register, set their availability as online or offline, and get discovered by owners nearby. Owners can easily search for labour based on city, area, and required skills.

Why Labour Finder?

  • ✔ Easy registration for labour & owners
  • ✔ Location-based labour search
  • ✔ Skill-based matching
  • ✔ OTP & password login
  • ✔ Fast and user-friendly
